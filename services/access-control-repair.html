Access Control Repair in Dallas-Fort Worth

Keypad not working? Intercom cutting out? Loop detector stuck open? We repair all gate access control systems across DFW — same-day service available.

Full Access Control Service

Every Type of Gate Access System

Your gate's access control system — the keypad, telephone entry, video intercom, or card reader — is the interface between your gate and everyone who needs to use it. When it fails, your gate effectively becomes useless even if the motor is running perfectly.

We repair and program all major access control brands and systems found in the DFW market. Whether it's a legacy telephone entry unit at a 1990s apartment complex or a modern Bluetooth/app-controlled residential gate, our technicians know how to get it working right.

Access Control Systems We Service

  • Keypad entry systems
  • Telephone entry / intercom
  • Video intercom systems
  • Key fob / proximity card readers
  • Vehicle loop detectors (inductive)
  • Wireless remote controls
  • Vehicle presence sensors
  • Bluetooth / smartphone entry
  • License plate readers (LPR)
  • Wiegand card systems

Common Problems We Fix

  • Keypad buttons not responding
  • Code accepted but gate won't open
  • Telephone entry not calling out
  • Intercom audio crackling or silent
  • Remote not triggering gate
  • Loop detector keeping gate open
  • New remote not pairing
  • Card/fob reader failure
Remote Programming We program and re-program remotes, keypads, and access codes for all major brands. Lost all your access codes? Technician won't leave until you have full access restored.
