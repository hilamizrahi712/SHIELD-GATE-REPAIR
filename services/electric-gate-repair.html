Electric Gate Repair in Dallas-Fort Worth

From a dead control board to a wiring short — our certified technicians diagnose and fix all electric gate issues across DFW. Same-day service available.

What We Fix

Every Type of Electric Gate Problem

Electric gates are complex systems. When one component fails — the control board, a transformer, a safety sensor — the whole gate can stop working. The good news: every electrical gate fault has a root cause and our technicians find it fast.

The DFW climate creates unique challenges for gate electronics. Extended summer heat above 100°F accelerates capacitor degradation and control board failure. The occasional hard freeze causes voltage fluctuations and condensation inside electrical panels. We've seen every Texas-specific failure pattern and know what to look for first.

Electric Gate Problems We Repair

  • Control board / logic board failure
  • Transformer and power supply issues
  • Safety sensor alignment and replacement
  • Wiring shorts and corrosion
  • Capacitor failure
  • Limit switch problems
  • Loop detector faults
  • Battery backup failure
  • Remote receiver problems
  • Timer and auto-close malfunction
  • Interlock circuit issues
  • Motor wiring burnout
Why Electric Diagnosis Takes Expertise Most electric gate problems mimic each other. A gate that "won't close" could be a sensor fault, a limit switch issue, or a board failure — and they all look the same from the outside. Our technicians use proper diagnostic equipment, not guesswork, to find the actual fault on the first visit.
