DKS Telephone Entry & Gate Specialists

DoorKing Systems Require More Than a Repair — They Require Programming

DoorKing (DKS) is the dominant telephone entry and access control brand in DFW’s multi-family residential market. The 1812 and 1815 series telephone entry systems are installed at thousands of apartment complexes, gated communities, and commercial properties across Dallas, Plano, Fort Worth, and the surrounding Metroplex. Unlike a standard gate motor, when a DKS system fails the problem is usually not mechanical — it’s electronic or programming-related.

That distinction matters. General gate technicians can swap a motor or adjust a limit switch. But when a DoorKing entry panel stops calling residents, starts dialing wrong numbers, or loses its entire directory after a power surge, the fix requires access to DoorKing’s proprietary DKML Manager software, the right programming hardware, and the knowledge to navigate the DKS system architecture. We carry all of it on every service call.

What We Fix on DoorKing Systems

📞
Entry Panel Not Calling Residents

The most common DKS failure mode in DFW. Could be the SIP/VoIP line, the call relay board, or a directory programming loss. We trace the full call path on-site and restore it.

💾
Directory & Database Loss

Power surges can wipe a DKS directory entirely. We back up, rebuild, and reprogram resident databases using DKML Manager — no sending the unit to a shop.

⚡
Control Board Failure

DKS boards damaged by lightning strikes or power fluctuations — we diagnose, source, and replace the most common DoorKing control boards.

🔒
Gate Not Releasing / Door Stuck

Relay not triggering when residents buzz visitors in — relay board diagnosis and replacement, or re-wiring the gate operator connection.

When DKS Fails at an Apartment

Why a Failed DoorKing System Is a Property Manager’s Emergency

A broken gate motor means the gate doesn’t move. A broken DKS system means none of your tenants can let visitors in — and your phone rings all day.

Residents Can’t Buzz In Guests

When the DKS call system fails, visitors can’t reach residents to get buzzed in. Delivery drivers, family members, and guests are all turned away. The property manager becomes the call center.

Package Theft Risk Increases

Many DFW apartment complexes rely on DKS relay integration to control package room and lobby access. When the system is down, staff has to manually manage entry — or security gaps open up.

Gate Stuck Open = Security Breach

A relay failure that locks the gate open is the worst-case scenario. Any vehicle can enter. Call us immediately — we treat stuck-open commercial gate calls as top priority regardless of time.

Lease Compliance Issues

Many DFW apartment leases contractually promise secured access. A prolonged DKS outage is a compliance issue, not just an inconvenience. Fast repair protects both tenants and the property owner.
