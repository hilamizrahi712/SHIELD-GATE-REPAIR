We Respond When You Need It Most

A broken gate is never convenient — but it's most serious when it leaves your property exposed at night, traps a vehicle, or locks out emergency services. That's why we staff technicians on-call across the entire DFW Metroplex 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Emergency Situations We Handle

  • Gate stuck in open position (security risk)
  • Gate stuck in closed position (access blocked)
  • Vehicle trapped inside gate
  • Gate hit by vehicle
  • Power outage causing gate failure
  • Gate off track or derailed
  • Gate arm broken or fallen
  • Storm / wind damage to gate
  • Gate motor fire or electrical fault
