Complete Gate Installation from Start to Finish

A new gate installation is more than putting up a gate and hanging a motor — it requires proper site assessment, correct operator sizing, professional electrical work, and careful calibration of every component. Done right, your gate runs reliably for years. Done wrong, you're calling for repairs every few months.

Shield Gate Repair handles the entire installation process: site visit and consultation, gate and operator selection, concrete work for posts, electrical connection, access control setup, and final programming and training. One call, one company, one installation done right.

FAQ

Gate Installation FAQs

A standard single-swing residential gate installation typically takes one full day. Dual-swing or slide gate systems with more complex wiring or concrete work may take 1–2 days. We give you a realistic timeline during the quote process so you can plan accordingly.
Residential automatic gate installations in DFW typically range from $3,000–$12,000+ depending on gate size, material, operator brand, and access control chosen. Commercial installations vary widely by scope. We provide a detailed quote after a site visit — there are no standard prices because no two driveways are the same.
Requirements vary by city in DFW. Some municipalities require a permit for gate and fence installations; others do not. We'll help you understand what your specific city requires and can assist with the permit process where applicable.
For residential swing gates, LiftMaster and US Automatic are our top recommendations — they're reliable in Texas heat, parts are readily available, and tech support is strong. For higher-end residential or commercial, we often recommend FAAC or Viking for their longevity. We'll recommend what's right for your specific use case.
