What to Expect

How a Genie Gate Repair Visit Goes

Most Genie repairs follow a clear sequence. Here’s what happens when we arrive at your property.

  1. 1.Read the blink code — the Genie LED tells us the fault category immediately. We know what each pattern means and head straight to the right component.
  2. 2.Test the motor directly — we bypass the control board and energize the motor independently to confirm whether the motor is good or failed.
  3. 3.Check the capacitor — if the motor tests good but won’t self-start, the capacitor is tested with a meter. Bad capacitor = swap and done.
  4. 4.Inspect the drive system — chain tension, sprocket alignment, and limit positions are verified before calling the repair complete.
  5. 5.Test all remotes and sensors — safety sensors, photo eyes, and all wireless remotes are tested before we leave.
  6. 6.Document and explain — we tell you exactly what failed, what we replaced, and what to watch for in the future.
