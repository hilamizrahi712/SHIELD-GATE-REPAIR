Iron Gate Repair in Dallas-Fort Worth

Bent frames, broken hinges, rust damage, missing pickets — we restore and repair ornamental iron gates throughout the DFW Metroplex. Quality welding and metalwork, same-day response available.

Structural & Ornamental Repair

Restoring Iron Gates to Like-New Condition

Ornamental iron gates are one of the most durable and beautiful entry solutions in DFW — but they're not immune to damage. Vehicle impacts, storm damage, ground settlement, and decades of corrosion from lawn sprinklers all take their toll. We repair and restore iron gates without the cost of full replacement.

Our technicians include experienced metal fabricators. We weld on-site, straighten bent frames, replace individual pickets or rails, and refinish treated surfaces — all in the field, not in a shop. This saves you time and significantly reduces the cost of restoring your gate.

Iron Gate Services We Provide

  • Welding — on-site, mobile welding
  • Frame straightening after vehicle impact
  • Rust removal and treatment
  • Primer and paint refinishing
  • Hinge replacement and adjustment
  • Latch and lock repair
  • Individual picket / bar replacement
  • Post re-setting and anchoring
  • Gate sag correction
  • Bottom clearance adjustment
DFW Sprinkler Rust Problem Iron gates in North Texas take a beating from irrigation systems. Sprinklers that hit gate frames repeatedly cause accelerated rust, particularly at the bottom rail and at welded joints. We treat the corrosion source, not just the surface, so the repair lasts.

Common Iron Gate Issues in DFW

  • Gate hit by vehicle (most common)
  • Gate sagging on hinges
  • Rust breakthrough at welds
  • Wind damage — bent or buckled
  • Broken or missing decorative elements
  • Post leaning from ground movement
