About Shield Gate Repair

DFW's dedicated gate repair specialists — not a general handyman, not a side-hustle. Gates are all we do, and it shows.

Our Story

Built for DFW. Built Around Gates.

Shield Gate Repair was founded on a straightforward observation: the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex is one of the fastest-growing regions in the United States, and thousands of new gated properties go up every year — but most gate repair calls still went to general handymen who had to look up the motor brand on their phone before they even opened the panel.

We changed that. Our technicians train exclusively on gate systems — swing gate operators, slide gate motors, underground hydraulic units, access control panels, intercom systems. When we show up at your property in Plano, Southlake, Fort Worth, or anywhere else across the Metroplex, we've already worked on dozens of gates exactly like yours.

DFW's climate isn't forgiving on gate systems. Summers that push past 100°F for weeks on end cook control boards and warp metal frames. The occasional hard freeze stresses hydraulic fluid and freezes digital keypads. Our technicians understand these regional patterns — they're not just gate-trained, they're Texas-trained.

Our Mission To deliver high-quality, affordable, and timely gate repair solutions that enhance security, convenience, and peace of mind for every DFW property owner we serve.
