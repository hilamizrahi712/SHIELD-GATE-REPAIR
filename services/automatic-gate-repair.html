Automatic Gate Repair in Dallas-Fort Worth

Swing gates, slide gates, dual gates, barrier arms — we repair all types of automatic gate systems across the entire DFW Metroplex. Same-day service available.

Full-System Repair

We Fix Every Type of Automatic Gate

Automatic gate systems are more complex than they look. A single gate involves a motor, control board, power supply, sensors, mechanical linkages, and an access control system — all working together. When something goes wrong, the fault can originate anywhere in the chain.

Our technicians are trained on the complete systems — electrical and mechanical. That means we find the actual root cause on the first visit, not the most obvious component that looks like the problem.

Gate Types We Service

  • Single swing gate systems
  • Dual swing gate systems
  • Slide / rolling gate systems
  • Cantilever slide gates
  • Barrier arm / parking gates
  • Vertical pivot lift gates
  • Solar-powered gate systems
  • Battery backup systems

Common Problems We Repair

  • Gate opens slowly or incompletely
  • Gate reverses before fully opening/closing
  • Squealing or grinding noise during operation
  • Gate arm binding or skipping
  • Gate doesn't respond to remote or keypad
  • Gate opens on its own unexpectedly
  • Gate drifts or doesn't stay closed
  • One panel works, the other doesn't
DFW-Specific Note Automatic gates in North Texas take extra abuse from the climate. Summer heat warps actuator arms and bakes motor electronics. Wind storms (common in DFW) bend gate frames and throw off alignment. After any major storm in the area, we see a surge in calls. We know exactly what to inspect first.
